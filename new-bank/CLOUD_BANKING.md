# Cloud Banking: Real-World Bank Cloud Migrations

This page highlights real banks that have successfully migrated to the cloud, with brief summaries and company logos for quick reference.

---

## Capital One

![Capital One Logo](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/7a/Capital_One_logo.svg)

- **Cloud Provider:** AWS
- **Summary:** Capital One was one of the first major U.S. banks to go all-in on AWS, migrating core workloads and closing data centers to improve agility, security, and innovation.
- **Reference:** [AWS Case Study](https://aws.amazon.com/solutions/case-studies/capital-one/)
